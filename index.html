      🌐 Welcome to Tc Coin Blockchain

      The Tc Coin Blockchain is a decentralized hybrid blockchain prototype designed to reward users for their time, trust, and sharing. Built on the principles of the Troanary Mirror Thesis, this platform integrates PWA support for easy installation, offline functionality, and seamless sharing to grow the community.

      ⚙️ Key Features

      • 🎰 Spin the Wheel to earn Tc Coins instantly.
      • 🏘️ Groups: Connect and collaborate in knowledge-driven networks.
      • 📜 Feed: View transactions and community updates in real time.
      • ❤️ Profile & Wallet: Manage your Tc balance, trust level, and personal info.
      • 📲 PWA Install: Install directly to your device for offline use.
      • 🚀 Share & Earn: Invite others and earn rewards for sharing.
      • 🔐 Decentralized: Fully independent, no central authority. You control your data.
